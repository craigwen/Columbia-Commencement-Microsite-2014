I began asking around on numerous internet platforms for anyone who might have more information about the video. None of them proved more useful than reddit.com/r/Syria. Where I was pointed to this website from Malaysia Arrahma.com According to @arrahmah it is a "A news site that focuses on World News with Islam."

One of the major focuses of my artistic practice is to engage a critical discussion about the source of our news. Community contributed and regulated sources like Reddit and Wikipedia are great places to find information where agendas tend to fail. I found little about Arrahmah.com on sources like these beyond a few links.

After translating the site (see below) it is clear this news outlet's allegiances are with the rebels. One might think who doesn't side with them? After all Assad is a brutal dictator. But like all things in the Middle East it is more complicated than identifying who is right or wrong. The Assad regime has it's allies and the roots of the bond run deep.

As an impartial observer I hope to see an end to the suffering of the Syrian citizens as quickly as possible. However the solution is as unclear as ever.
